<p>
    <ruby data-en="Indonesia is expanding coral reef restoration">印尼正在扩大珊瑚礁修复工作<rt>Yìnní zhèngzài kuòdà shānhújiāo xiūfù gōngzuò</rt></ruby>，
    <ruby data-en="local teams place reef structures in sea areas">本地团队在海里放置修复结构<rt>běndì tuánduì zài hǎilǐ fàngzhì xiūfù jiégòu</rt></ruby>

</p>

<p>

</p>

<p>
